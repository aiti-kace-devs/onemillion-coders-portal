title: 'What is One Million Coders?'
answer: 'The One Million Coders programme is a transformative four-year initiative launched by the government of Ghana to train one million young Ghanaians in advanced ICT skills, fostering a robust digital workforce. This initiative, spearheaded by the Ministry of Communications and Digital Technology Innovation (MoCDTI), in collaboration with the Ghana Investment Fund for Electronic Communications (GIFFEC), the National Information Technology Agency (NITA), Ghana Digital Centre and the training and implementation partner, Ghana-India Kofi Annan Centre of Excellence in ICT (GI-KACE). The programme offers training benchmarked to international certification standards.'
